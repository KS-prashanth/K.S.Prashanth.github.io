About Me

I am Karnati Sai Prashanth, and my journey into the world of technology and data science has been a lifelong adventure. My fascination with technology, particularly computers, began at a young age, initially driven by gaming but ultimately fueled by the profound impact of computing systems on our society. Growing up in a politically inclined family, my exposure to statistics and data further ignited my interest in the power of information. Pursuing a Bachelor of Technology degree in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, India, provided me with the technical foundation I needed.

During my undergraduate years, I immersed myself in academic and extracurricular activities, developing a diverse skill set that includes programming languages, mathematical concepts, and data visualization tools. My passion for data science only grew stronger as I engaged in high-impact research and collaborated on projects in fields like speech processing and natural language processing. I have consistently excelled academically and actively contributed to my university community, making me a positive addition to any institution/Company.
